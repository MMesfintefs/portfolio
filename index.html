Story

Origins

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Michael Mesfin Tefera was born and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He completed his primary and secondary education at Ethio-Parents’ School (Gerji Campus), where he developed an early interest in business, economics, and systems thinking. Growing up in a business-oriented household, he was exposed early to finance, operations, and decision-making through his parents’ work. This environment, combined with strong academic preparation in economics and business during high school, shaped his foundational business acumen.

Move to the U.S.

Fall 2021

In Fall 2021, Michael moved to the United States to pursue undergraduate studies in International Business. During his academic progression, he consistently took heavy course loads and later expanded his studies by adding Management Information Systems and Business Administration as additional majors, driven by a desire to integrate business strategy with technology.

The through-line

Systems + Analytics

Alongside business, Michael maintained a long-standing curiosity for technology. Despite limited infrastructure access during his early years, he gravitated toward understanding how systems worked—often learning visually by observing, disassembling, and reconstructing devices. This analytical curiosity evolved into a structured interest in data, systems, and decision-making. Through his academic and professional experiences, Michael became increasingly focused on data-driven decision-making, risk mitigation, and governance. He views analytics not as an abstract technical skill, but as a practical tool for managing uncertainty, improving outcomes, and enabling sustainable business performance.
