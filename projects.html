Credit Default Risk Prediction

Developed a credit risk prediction model using lending data from over 300,000 applicants. Includes data preprocessing, feature engineering, logistic regression baseline modeling, and ROC-AUC evaluation. Uncovering the strongest signals of borrower default risk

Compensation Drivers in STEM Careers

Investigated compensation patterns across 62,000+ STEM salary records to understand what influences earnings in technical careers. Used regression modeling and statistical analysis in R to explore relationships between education, experience, and career mobility.

AI Disruption & Automation Risk Analysis

Analyzed workforce data to explore how AI and automation may reshape occupations by 2030. Built visual analytics in Tableau with statistical validation in R to examine how job structure and skills relate to automation exposure.
